\section{Intended Market of Product}

The intended market of the proposed product consists of educational institutions that conduct supervised practical examinations in computer laboratories and require stronger operational control, monitoring, and post-exam accountability \cite{pmc_cbt}.

\subsection{Target Audience}
The primary target audience includes universities, colleges, and departments offering programs in computer science, software engineering, information technology, cybersecurity, data science, and related technical disciplines \cite{eassess1}. These institutions regularly conduct lab-based examinations where students work on institutional machines and produce digital outputs within a fixed time window. They need a system that not only delivers an exam but also supports role management, student verification, monitoring, submission handling, result management, and reporting.

\subsection{Institutional Environment}
A second important market segment includes academic training centers, skill-development institutes, certification labs, and departmental examination cells that manage scheduled practical tests for batches of students \cite{mit_cbt}. Such environments require machine allocation, attendance control, restricted usage, and evidence-based review of exam sessions. The proposed system is suitable for these environments because it is designed around structured lab operations rather than general classroom administration alone.
